Ronesha D. Dennis

Web Developer and Non-Profit Programming Professional

Ronesha D. Dennis is a graduate of Howard University's John H. Johnson School of Communications. She holds a degree in Journalism with a minor in Computer Science. Shortly after graduating, Ronesha discovered that being a journalist would not fulfill her need to help others, and instead, spent five years working at The National Campaign to Prevent Teen and Unplanned Pregnancy, with the latter half of her time at the nonprofit spent developing relationships with respected organizations in the African-American community. Though Ronesha felt her work was purposeful, her passion for programming and web development, which had been an interest since well before entering college, could not be satiated. She completed Tech Talent South's Web Development Immersive bootcamp in May 2016, and is currently looking for projcts to expand her technological skills.
